Common Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Is the free website really free? +
Yes, 100%. You get a professional, mobile-optimized website with a custom subdomain at no cost — no credit card, no trial period, no hidden fees. We offer this because we want to be your long-term partner, and a great website is the starting point.
Can I use my own domain name? +
Absolutely. You can connect your own custom domain (like yourshop.com) to your free website. We'll walk you through the DNS setup — it takes about 5 minutes.
Do I need both services? +
Not at all. The free website and the Sales Hub work independently. Many shops start with just the website and add the Sales Hub later when they're ready to level up their operations. Use what you need.
Can I import my existing customers? +
Yes. You can import customers via CSV from any existing system — spreadsheets, Square, Jobber, whatever you're using now. Our onboarding team can even help you migrate the data for free.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your data is always yours. If you cancel the Sales Hub, you can export all your customer data, invoices, and job records anytime. Your free website stays live regardless.
I'm not tech-savvy. Can I still use this? +
That's exactly who we built this for. If you can use Instagram, you can use this platform. And our support team is always available to help — we'll even set up your website for you if you want.
